Thank you for using Color Roles — a tasteful role-coloring utility that helps servers look and feel cohesive. By using the service you agree to these Terms. Be cool. Don’t abuse it.

2. What We Provide

The Bot posts tasteful color selections and assigns roles on button or reaction. Each role is a visual label — nothing more. The service includes: curated palettes, role assignment, role removal, and the /tos page you’re reading now.

3. Acceptable Use
